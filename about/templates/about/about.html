 Do the great things while they are easy and do the great things while they are small. A journey of a thousand miles must begin with a single step. 

— Lao Tzu

To say that the road to Chew The Rag Clothing Co. is a journey is an understatement. What started as an idea has grown into a passion. A passion to organically grow a business that provides quality clothes at a reasonable price, making them accessible to people from all walks of life. This has started a journey to build Chew The Rag Clothing Co. step-by-step. We are taking our time to ensure that it is done the right way. One product at a time we endeavour to find the best quality materials for our products leading on the way to fulfil our promise to you, our customers.

Why clothing? Clothing is an expression of one's sense of style. Clothing is a necessity. If you are like most of us, then you develop a relation to some of your clothes. The favourite shirt. The one item of clothing you just can’t bear to dispose of no matter what shape it is in and no matter how much your friends and loved ones beg you to get rid of it. No matter what others say about it. That one shirt that has grown to be the most comfortable item in your closet that you wear every chance you get regardless of how much attention it garners. That shirt that you protect like a best friend. Chew The Rag Clothing Co. endeavours to produce that item of clothing that stands the test of time, that you wear with pride and becomes the best friend throughout the years to come.

At Chew The Rag Clothing Co. we love clothing. We don’t want to provide clothing that you put in the closet and wear the odd Friday or Saturday. We want you to wear our clothes often, with pride. With that goal in mind, we continue to provide a level of quality with our clothing that provides you with an equal level of comfort and style.
